Not to be taken as a push to do away with aspects of institutional patriarchy that on one hand celebrate women (the wives at least) as equal partners to husbands, and on the other prevents them from fully enjoying that partnership in all other aspects of the Church. It's just a conversation. :)

Ordain Women aspires to create a space for Mormons to articulate issues of gender inequality they may be hesitant to raise alone. As a group we intend to put ourselves in the public eye and call attention to the need for the ordination of Mormon women to the priesthood.
